# Mythical Village. [WIP]

Language: 

- Python
  
Genre: 

- Text-based RPG

Lore: 

- You wake up in a destroyed, abandoned-looking village. The village is in a magical sphere blocking your escape. You have a vision of a humanoid creature which confuses you further. You decide to explore the village and answer many unexplained questions you had since you woke up.

Final Goal:
  
- Dungeon Crawler type RPG game. Fully text based with a story line. Custom races with unique stats and play styles. Custom classes allowing unique abilities depending on the class and race. Unique stats (Damage, Weight, Defence, Accuracy). Multiple layers for levels. AI Chat NPCs (chat bot style). Randomly generated encounters. Large library of items. Large dungeon-style map. More functionality to come
